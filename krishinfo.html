Welcome to Krishi Info: Your Source for Agricultural Insights

The N.B Krishi Farm is located in Malarani-5 Bangi Baghari, Arghakganchi, Nepal. Established in the year 2070, the N.B Krishi Farm is a family-driven endeavor.

At Krishi Info, we're dedicated to providing you with the latest and most valuable information in the world of agriculture and farming. Whether you're a seasoned farmer or just getting started, our platform is here to empower you with knowledge, tips, and resources to make your agricultural journey a success.

Explore our wide range of articles, guides, and expert advice covering topics such as crop cultivation, livestock management, sustainable farming practices, market trends, and much more. Our mission is to support farmers in making informed decisions that lead to higher yields, healthier crops, and thriving farms.

Join us in nurturing a greener future and reaping the rewards of your hard work. Together, we can cultivate not just crops, but also knowledge and prosperity.

Happy farming!
